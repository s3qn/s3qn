## 👤 About Me

Offensive security practitioner with hands-on experience across **Windows**, **Linux**, and **web application** environments. I execute methodical penetration tests — from reconnaissance through exploitation and privilege escalation — and deliver evidence-backed reports that guide real remediation. I've performed full environmental compromises within controlled engagements and continuously refine my methodology through lab work and live platforms.

Currently working as a **Network Security Validation Engineer at Larch Networks**, where I test applications, networks, and authentication workflows daily.
